About Bayley

Bayley is a wife and stay at home mother of 2 children. She is dedicated and determined in whatever it is she sets her mind to, often pursuing research and growth through reading and community to better herself and others. Bayley prefers to be around others and always seeks to be in a people filled environment with her Choleric/Sanguine personality. She enjoys thrift store shopping to find the best deal, crafting, reading, cooking, and interior design. Her favorite design style is Modern Organic, which brings elements of simple and natural styles together. Bayley has some interests in music and would really love to someday take a crash course in piano to be able to accompany herself singing. Of all of her hobbies, thrifting is probably her biggest passion. If she could, she would create a business out of this, to find the best deals and get them to others.
